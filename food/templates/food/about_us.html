About Us

Welcome to Royal Taj, your home for authentic Indian and Pakistani cuisine in Pendik and Kurtkoy! With a legacy spanning four years, we take pride in bringing the true essence of Indian and Pakistani dishes to your plate.

🌟 Our Mission:

At Royal Taj, we strive for excellence and aim to deliver a taste experience that goes beyond the ordinary. Our commitment to quality ingredients and traditional recipes ensures each dish is a celebration of flavor.

👩‍🍳 Chef's Specials:

Explore a diverse menu curated by our skilled chefs who infuse passion into every creation. From classic favorites to innovative delights, there's something to satisfy every palate.

🎓 Student Subsidies:

We understand the importance of good food on a student's journey. That's why we've introduced subsidized rates exclusively for Sabanci students. Quality meals at affordable prices - because great taste should be accessible to everyone.

📍 Our Locations:

Visit us in Pendik and Kurtkoy to experience a culinary journey that transports you to the heart of India and Pakistan. Our warm and inviting ambiance complements the richness of our dishes.

🤝 Thank You for Four Wonderful Years:

As we celebrate four years of serving you, we express our gratitude for your continued support. Join us at Royal Taj, where every meal is a celebration of tradition, taste, and togetherness.
